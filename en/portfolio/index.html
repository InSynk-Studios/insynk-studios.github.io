HOW WE WORK

Our Work Process

From ideating to building a fully functional MVP, here is what our work process looks like. (The timeline for the processes are listed)

1.

Requirement Analysis & Ideating the Solution

Here, we try to understand the exact vision of our client, and try to align our ideas with their their's. It is import to bring alignment.

2.

Wireframing and Storyboarding the Solution

In this stage, we start wireframing the solution and storyboarding the wireframes to get an early look of what the prototype might look like.

3.

Feedback & Iteration on Prototype

Here, we start iterating and gathering feedback over the wireframes and storyboards, in order to further improve the solution and see it aligns with the problem.

4.

Delivering an Interactive Prototype

[Estimated time till here is 5-8 Weeks] In this stage, we create the first clickable prototype of the solution. Prototypes include mockups, and product specs of the important components.

5.

Developing Proof of Concept

[Estimated time till here is 9-12 Weeks] After having the interative mockups in hand, we do a technology analysis and build out the first functional prototype with key feature sets. This can be used to check technical feasibility and collect initial data for building a new business case.

6.

Developing MVP

[Estimated time till here is 14-18 Weeks] A proof of concept acts as a building block for the Pilot MVP. Here we iterate over the insights and imporve the product features. We build out the remaining important components and constantly validate the problem & it's solution.
